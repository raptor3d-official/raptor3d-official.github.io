Industries Impacted By 3D Printing

3D printing is revolutionizing the industry and is the future ahead.

Industry's Future Is Here

3D printing is revolutionizing the industry and is the future ahead.

Product R&D

Numerous leading product designers have already integrated the 3D printing in their product research and development stage. In modern product design, 3D printing has taken up the responsibility of being an end-to-end and cost-effective prototyping solution.

Prototyping

3D printed consumer product design allows for rapid prototyping in the pre-manufacturing stage. Time saved in the prototyping stage helps businesses manufacture quicker and reach markets faster. Overall, manufacturers enjoy the benefits of lower costs and added agility.

Die and Mould

In Die & Mould industry engineers undergo a lot of iterations in design of physical tool which is a very time consuming and costly process. 3D printing cut the time and cost by providing a pre-production prototype just from the CAD Design therby confirming various aspects.

Casting

Most industrial manufacturers are aware of the tedious and time-consuming nature of pattern casting. Apart from this, pattern casting is labour-intensive and subject to higher tolerance variations. 3D printed pattern casting can help solve these production challenges. Sand casting patterns can be 3D printed easily with FDM Technology.

Architecture

Architects, Engineers, and Construction (AEC) professionals understand the paramount importance of accurate and tangible scale models. It helps them and their clients visualize ideas realistically and vividly. Traditional ways of designing and assembling scale-models are tedious, costly, and rely heavily on handful skilled craftsmen.

Corporate Gifting

Maintaining relations with allies is very important for corporates. Whether its launching a new product or a gift for a wonderful occasion, we provide a medium of highly customized gift tailored according to the brand and occasion. Introducing new products to allies, distributors and marketers has been simplified by 3D Printing miniature models.

Education

Education holds the key to unlocking the true potential of human ingenuity. Education should be multi-dimensional, giving equal importance to theoretical as well as a hands-on, practical approach. Integrating 3D printing in education exposes eager learners and future creators to stay adept with tomorrow’s cutting-edge technology.

Low-Volume Manufacturing

In this new era of start-ups, smart entrepreneurs know the importance of MVP (Minimum Viable Product) to verify their assumptions in the real market. We provide this unique solution by manufacturing small quantities of their product which look close to the actual production product to verify their assumptions before investing into the tooling.
